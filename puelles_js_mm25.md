# Molecular Insight into the Structure of Poly(Ionic Liquids) for Sodium Metal Battery Applications

## Presenting Author
Jhonatan Soto Puelles

## Authors
Jhonatan Soto Puelles, Kewei Cai, Maria Forsyth, Fangfang Chen

## Abstract
Cationic poly(ionic liquids) (PolyILs), with ionic liquid cations attached to polymer backbones, have attracted significant attention in recent years, due to their potential to simultaneously achieve high ionic conductivity and high metal-ion transference numbers.
